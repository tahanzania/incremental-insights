How Incremental Insights Works

1. Purpose

This tool helps media teams identify incremental budget opportunities by finding high-performing campaigns that are capped on budget (pacing at ~100%). It calculates how much additional spend could be absorbed based on the remaining flight days.

2. Upload Data

Upload a standard Excel or CSV export from your campaign management platform. The tool processes everything locally in your browser—no data is uploaded to any server.

  • Required Columns: Partner, Advertiser, Campaign, Decision Power Score, Daily Incremental Budget, Pacing, Days Remaining.
  • KPI Columns: KPI Type, Average KPI Value, Goal Value (for performance vs. goal analysis).

3. Analysis Logic

The tool filters for "Incremental Opportunities" based on strict criteria:

  • High Pacing: The campaign must be pacing at or above the threshold (Default: 99%). This indicates it is fully utilizing its current daily budget.
  • High Decision Power: The campaign must have a Decision Power Score above the selected threshold (Default: 50). This score represents the AI's confidence in the campaign's performance signal.

Formula: Opportunity = Daily Incremental Budget × Remaining Flight Days

4. KPI Performance

The tool evaluates if a campaign is beating its KPI goal:

  • CPA/Cost Metrics: considered "Beating" if the Average Value is lower than the Goal.
  • CTR/VCR/Reach: considered "Beating" if the Average Value is higher than the Goal.

5. Actions

Use the Email Builder to generate pre-formatted templates (Executive Summary, Action Required, etc.) to request budget approval from stakeholders.
